Enemies

Enemies are npcs in the world that will attack the player or can be attacked by the player. The auto-fight checkbox will determine whether the player will automatically enter combat on entering the area the enemy is.

Descriptions

As with items and friends, enemies have two descriptions, one that will be used when talking to the friend, and one that is used to describe them being in a room.

Damage

The creation of an Enemy requires defining the damage they can do. A minimum and maximum damage must be defined, from which a random number between them will be selected every time they attack. If you want the enemy to do a fixed amount of damage the values can be set to the same.

Drops

Once created, items can be added to the enemy that will be dropped in the location on defeat.

Example

Your world has the player go to a bandit encampment to retrieve a kidnapped member of the royal family. You create a Bandit Leader enemy that the player is required to fight on entering the camp. In order to open the cage a key is required and after creating the key and the bandit leader you add the key to their list of drops.
